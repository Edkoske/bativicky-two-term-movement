Who We Are

A Grassroots Political Movement

Founded in December 2024, the TUTAM Movement is a grassroots political movement established by Bati Vicky. We believe in President William Samoei Ruto's vision of transforming Kenya through a Broad-Based Government and inclusive development that leaves no mwananchi behind.

TUTAM Movement exists to promote unity, defend truth, and ensure that government programs and opportunities reach the people they are meant to serve. We are committed to supporting the implementation of President Ruto's development agenda, countering misinformation, and mobilizing citizens toward continuity and stability in 2027.

Our movement is built on the legacy of the Hustlers Youth Movement and UDA Babes, which Bati Vicky founded in 2022. These initiatives played a significant role in mobilization efforts that supported the current administration and created leadership pathways for the youth.

TUTAM FORCES MOVEMENT

A Grassroots Political Movement

Founded in December 2024, the TUTAM FORCES MOVEMENT is a grassroots political movement established by Bati Vicky. We believe in President William Samoei Ruto's vision of transforming Kenya through a Broad-Based Government and inclusive development that leaves no mwananchi behind.

TUTAM FORCES MOVEMENT exists to promote unity, defend truth, and ensure that government programs and opportunities reach the people they are meant to serve. We are committed to supporting the implementation of President Ruto's development agenda, countering misinformation, and mobilizing citizens toward continuity and stability in 2027.

Our movement is built on the legacy of the Hustlers Youth Movement and UDA Babes, which Bati Vicky founded and patronized in 2022. As the Founder and Patron of these groups, Bati Vicky played a key role in mobilizing youth and young women to participate in voter registration and to support President William Ruto in his election as the 5th President of the Republic of Kenya. Both groups also spearheaded peace rallies across every county, promoting unity, peaceful coexistence, and active civic participation throughout the campaign period. These initiatives created leadership pathways for the youth and supported the current administration.
